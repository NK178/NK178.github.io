My Hobbies

Hobbies are great source of relief and enjoyment for one. Here are some of my favourites:

Gaming is a easy way to stress relief. Just hop on any game and just play, it helps to keep the mind blank .
Avoid getting too frustrated at your games.
My favourites is Team Fortress 2. My favourite class is the Demoman.

Sports

Time and time again, Sports have shown to release dopamine and improves one's cardiovascular health.
My favourite sports are Badminton and Football

Chess

Chess both trains the cognitive function in our brains while letting other parts rest
My favourite chess opening is the Vienna Game.
